How It Works

Synex runs a 24-hour hourly simulation of your energy system — modelling solar production, battery charge/discharge, and grid draw for every hour of a representative day.

  • Solar generation based on regional irradiance data
  • Battery modelled with round-trip efficiency losses
  • Grid usage calculated to fill any remaining demand gap
  • ROI computed against a grid-only baseline scenario
